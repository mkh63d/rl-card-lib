Caveats that apply throughout

  • Evaluation seeds the global RNG once per episode, so before/after evaluations perturb the training RNG stream. This is deterministic and reproducible, but results depend on where evaluations are placed.
  • Klondike reports cards to the foundation as its headline because reward shaping has been changed since earlier runs and cards-up is invariant to it.
  • Only the most recent run of each model is stored; re-running a model replaces its record, figures and checkpoints.
